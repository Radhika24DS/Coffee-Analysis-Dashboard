☕ Coffee Analysis via Coastal Area Preference – Power BI Dashboard
1. Project Title / Headline

Coastal Coffee Analytics: Regional Preference and Sales Insights Dashboard

An interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze coffee sales patterns across different coastal regions. This visualization tool helps identify regional preferences, product performance, and geographic trends influencing coffee consumption and sales.

2. Short Description / Purpose

The Coastal Coffee Analytics Dashboard is a dynamic and visually rich Power BI report developed to explore how coastal location influences coffee sales, product demand, and customer purchasing behavior. The dashboard focuses on analyzing sales distribution, regional performance, and product category trends across multiple coastal areas. This tool is intended for business analysts, sales managers, and decision-makers to understand geographic sales patterns, optimize product distribution, and support data-driven business strategies.

3. Tech Stack

The dashboard was built using the following tools and technologies:

Power BI Desktop – Main data visualization platform used to design interactive reports and dashboards.

Power Query – Used for data cleaning, transformation, and preparation of raw sales data.

DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) – Used to create calculated measures such as Total Sales, Regional Sales %, and Performance KPIs.

Data Modeling – Relationships established between sales, location, and product tables for accurate analysis.

File Formats – .pbix for dashboard development and .png for dashboard previews.

4. Data Source

Source: Coffee Shop Sales Dataset (Excel format)

The dataset contains detailed transactional records of coffee sales, including:

Transaction ID

Transaction Date and Time

Store ID and Store Location (Coastal regions)

Product Category, Product Type, and Product Detail

Quantity Sold

Unit Price

Total Sales Revenue

The dataset includes over 149,000 sales records, enabling comprehensive analysis of sales trends, regional preferences, and product performance across coastal areas.

5. Features / Highlights
• Business Problem

Coffee businesses operating across coastal regions need to understand how geographic location influences sales performance, customer preferences, and product demand. Without proper visualization and analysis, it is difficult to identify high-performing regions, optimize inventory distribution, and make strategic business decisions.

• Goal of the Dashboard

The main objective of this dashboard is to:

Analyze coffee sales performance across coastal regions

Identify top-performing locations and products

Understand regional customer preferences

Track sales trends over time

Support business decision-making using visual analytics

• Walkthrough of Key Visuals

KPI Cards:
Display overall business performance metrics such as Total Sales Revenue, Total Quantity Sold, and Total Transactions. These indicators provide a quick overview of overall performance.

Sales Trend Line Chart:
Shows how coffee sales change over time, helping identify growth trends, seasonal patterns, and peak sales periods.

Regional Sales Bar Chart:
Compares total sales across different coastal locations, helping identify high-performing and low-performing regions.

Product Category Analysis Chart:
Visualizes sales distribution across product categories such as Coffee, Tea, and other beverages, highlighting the most popular categories.

Product Performance Chart:
Shows which specific products generate the highest revenue and customer demand.

Location-Based Analysis:
Helps understand geographic sales distribution and coastal area preference patterns.

• Business Impact & Insights

This dashboard helps businesses:

Identify top-performing coastal regions

Understand customer product preferences

Optimize product distribution and inventory

Improve sales strategies based on regional demand

Enable data-driven decision-making

By transforming raw sales data into meaningful visual insights, this dashboard provides valuable intelligence to improve operational efficiency and business growth.
